Doc and Raven's Babies

On March 4th 2019 Doc and Raven had 11 pups. Nine survived.






Doc

Doc is a Belgian Malinois, AKC from the KPNV working line. He is 5 years old.

Raven

Raven is a purebreed black German Shepherd with no pedigree. She is 2 years old.

Angel

Angel is spunky, defiant and very smart.

Caleb

Caleb is very submissive and doesn't like to be alone.

Champ

Champ is a high energy puppy with a strong prey drive.

Chloe

Chloe is a very sweet girl that loves to cuddle.

Greta

Greta is a medium energy pup, who is extremly intelligent and eager to please.

Jesse

Jesse is a happy puppy that loves people and loves to chew.

Niko

Niko is very energetic and learns very quickly, he is brave and fearless.

Tamutzu

Tamutzu is a very shy puppy. He is quiet and keeps to himself.

Yoshi

Yoshi is a high energy spunky little girl. She has a high prey drive and loves to play.
